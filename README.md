 **Details of 3 best Hill stations**


1.**Ooty:**Nicknamed the "Queen of Hill Stations," famous for its pleasant climate, botanical gardens, tea gardens, and the Nilgiri Mountain Railway.


<imag.src="https://pixabay.com/photos/bird-fauna-roof-relax-view-9920902/">



2.**Munnar:**Celebrated for its expansive tea and coffee plantations, misty valleys, waterfalls, and the Eravikulam National Park.


<img.src="https://pixabay.com/photos/candles-candlelight-tea-lights-9874140/">



3.**Kodaikanal:**A serene lakeside resort town in the Palani Hills, known as the "Princess of Hill Stations".
